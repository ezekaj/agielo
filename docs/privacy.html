Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

Z.E Digital Tech ("we", "us", "our") operates the Human Cognition AI website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, demo, and API.

2. Information We Collect

Website visits: We use GitHub Pages for hosting, which may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referring page, timestamps). We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking scripts beyond what is required for Tailwind CSS CDN and Google Fonts.

API usage: When you use the simulation API, we may log request timestamps and scenario types for service monitoring. We do not log personal information through the API.

Sponsorship: If you become a sponsor via PayPal, IBAN, or cryptocurrency, payment information is processed by the respective payment provider. We receive only the information necessary to fulfill sponsorship benefits (name, email, payment confirmation).

Contact: If you email us at elvizekaj@zedigital.tech, we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the website and API
  • To fulfill sponsorship benefits (listing names/logos as agreed)
  • To respond to support requests and inquiries
  • To monitor and improve service reliability

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., displaying sponsor names/logos)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of Z.E Digital Tech and its users

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting (GitHub's privacy practices)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN: Stylesheet delivery
  • Google Fonts: Font delivery (Google's privacy policy)
  • Fly.io: API hosting for the cognitive simulation engine
  • PayPal: Payment processing for sponsorships

6. Data Retention

We retain sponsorship records for the duration of the sponsorship plus a reasonable period for accounting purposes. API logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days. Email correspondence is retained as long as necessary to address your inquiry.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future communications
  • Request removal of your name/logo from sponsor listings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at elvizekaj@zedigital.tech.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, including using HTTPS for all web traffic and secure payment processing through established providers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us to have it removed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: elvizekaj@zedigital.tech
